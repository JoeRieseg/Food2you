Introduction

Food2You is a newly opened restaurant in 136 Rue Du Faubourg Poissonniere Paris, Ile-de-paris .This app helps you save money and reduce your food waste by offering ready prep meals C2C. When you have a leftover plate that you won't use you can list it here and someone around you will purchase it. Meals are between 2€to5€.

Entree

Athenian Leg of Lamb

Athenian Leg of Lamb, is full of robust Greek flavors. Garlic, lemon, and herbs compliment the lamb so well and the meat is so soft and tender and falling off the bone.

Bistecca Alla Fiorentina

The steak is typically from Chianina cattle - an ancient Tuscan breed known for its prized and tasty meat - seasoned with local spices and grilled over red-hot coals. It's traditionally served "rare", but we won't judge if you ask for medium. (Not too much, anyway).

Rosemary Focaccia

This delicious Rosemary Focaccia Bread recipe is easy to make, and topped with fresh rosemary, olive oil and flaky sea salt.

Bocconcini Pizza
